## What is CobranzaDigital?

CobranzaDigital is a full-stack POS platform built with **Angular 21** on the frontend and **.NET** on the backend. It provides businesses with a complete solution to operate physical stores — from opening a cash register shift, to ringing up sales with customizable products, to closing the day with a full cash reconciliation.

The platform is designed as a **multi-tenant** system, allowing a single deployment to serve multiple independent businesses (tenants), each with their own stores, catalog, inventory, and user base.

## System overview

CobranzaDigital is structured around three levels of access:

| Level | Roles | Scope |
|-------|-------|-------|
| **Platform** | `SuperAdmin` | Manages all tenants and stores across the platform |
| **Tenant Admin** | `AdminStore`, `Manager` | Manages catalog, inventory, and staff within their tenant |
| **Cashier** | `Cashier` | Operates the POS terminal — sales and shifts |
